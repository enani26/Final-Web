Welcome to Pizzeria Restaurant

Pizzeria About Us: Where Pizza Meets Burgers in Perfect Harmony Welcome to Pizzeria About Us, a culinary haven that celebrates the best of both worlds - the delectable allure of pizza and the irresistible charm of burgers. Our restaurant is more than just a dining destination; it's a gastronomic adventure that promises to tantalize your taste buds and delight your senses. Our Story: A Fusion of Flavors At Pizzeria About Us, we believe that the best meals are those shared with loved ones. Inspired by our passion for culinary creativity and a love for both Italian and American cuisines, we set out on a quest to craft a unique dining experience that bridges cultures and celebrates the art of fusion. Our journey begins in the heart of Italy, where we draw inspiration from the traditional Neapolitan pizza - a timeless classic that embodies the essence of simplicity and authenticity. Handcrafted with the finest ingredients, our pizzas boast a thin, crispy crust, topped with a symphony of flavors that pay homage to Italy's rich culinary heritage.

Our Chef

At Pizzeria About Us, our culinary journey is guided by none other than our esteemed and masterful chef - the Maestro of Culinary Creations. With a passion for flavor, an unwavering dedication to craftsmanship, and a flair for innovation, our chef is the driving force behind the delightful symphony of tastes that grace our menu.

Franco pepe

masterful Chef

Meet Franco Pepe, an acclaimed chef and the culinary genius behind some of the most sought-after Neapolitan pizzas in the world. Hailing from the pizza capital of Naples, Italy, Franco's passion for pizza-making is deeply rooted in his family's longstanding tradition.

Tony Gemignani

Neapolitan Chef

Tony Gemignani is a renowned and award-winning pizzaiolo hailing from San Francisco, California. With over three decades of experience, Tony is a 13-time World Pizza Champion and a true legend in the world of pizza-making. His pizzas are celebrated for their perfect crusts, diverse styles, and artful toppings. Beyond his culinary mastery, Tony is a charismatic teacher, author, and ambassador for pizza culture.

Chris Bianco

Neapolitan Chef

Chris Bianco is an esteemed pizzaiolo and restaurateur based in Phoenix, Arizona. Widely regarded as one of America's top pizza makers, Chris's passion for pizza began at a young age. With an unwavering commitment to quality and simplicity, he has mastered the art of crafting exceptional Neapolitan-style pizzas.
